    Industrial Design (ID)

    An Industrial Design (ID) protection is another fundamental area of Intellectual Property Law and Practice. The objective of the Design law in India is to strike a balance between protection of registered designs and promotion of design activity.
    The Industrial Design of an item in this context refers to the combination of lines, contours and/or shape that make up its outer appearance. It can also include its colour, texture, materials and/or its surface decoration. From a commercial point of view, it can be thought of as the visual impact a product makes on the consumer.
    The Industrial Design protection is important as design enables the consumer to choose a product on the basis of aesthetics and to recognise that product over other similar items on the market. The Industrial Design can strongly affect the success of a product. The Industrial design protection is available to industrial products, handicrafts, technical and medical instruments, watches and jewellery, houseware, electrical appliances, automotive products, etc.
