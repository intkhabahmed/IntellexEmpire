    Insurance Law

    Insurance is financial protection against untoward or unexpected incidents. Organizations have to get insured against various risks and also provide insurance to employees. In some cases, they have to provide insurance cover to clients as well. There are different types of insurance - life insurance, fire insurance, medical insurance, general liability insurance, professional liability insurance, etc.
