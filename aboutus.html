    About IntellexEmpire

    Organisation Profile:
    IntellexEmpire offers a wide range of legal solutions with a constructive and focused techno-legal approach. We aim to work with our clients with a focus on their strategic business objectives. Professionalism, integrity, and cost optimization are ingrained in every solution that we deliver to the client.

    Affiliations
    IntellexEmpire has strong local and international affiliations with law firms and with firms having relevant specialized expertise so that we can provide access to the best legal minds and technical experts to our clients. With our extensive worldwide network of associates, we can take charge of cases related to national and international IP filing, trans-border litigation, global financial transactions, etc.

    Careers
    IntellexEmpire aims for legal excellence. We seek to recruit and retain talented professionals who share our aim. We have opportunities for new as well as experienced lawyers, scientists, engineers, and IP experts. We are also hiring support staff such as paralegals and administrative officers. Prior experience in the field of Intellectual Property is not mandatory but would be an advantage.
    We want to put together a team that is highly skilled, motivated and has strong communication skills. We are committed to providing a complete workplace for our employees to work, learn, collaborate, and grow.
    Our current job openings are

    • Patent Agent - Patent Drafting, Patent Prosecution - Engineering, Pune, Full Time
    • Paralegal - Indian Filing, Foreign Filing, Formalities & Prosecution, Pune, Full Time
    • Advocate - IPR advoacte

    Internships
    We believe in giving young students opportunities to gain experience in the field of Intellectual Property. Students selected as interns will be incorporated into a team based on their background and interest. We appreciate passion for work, hard work and the ability to learn quickly.
    Students can apply for internships with a resume and a covering letter to hr@intellexempire.com with subject - 'Internship'.

    Our Clients
