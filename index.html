    About Intellex Empire


    Organisation Profile: IntellexEmpire is an organisation that offers a constructive and focused approach to our clients for fulfilling their requirements, their targets and strategic business objectives and business goals.

    Our Dedicated Team: Our values drive us to serve our clients with Honesty, Integrity, Passion, Dedication and Knowledge with the help of our dedicated team comprising technically and legally sound experts to deliver tailor made quality technical and legal solutions to each client. We have a team of Technology Experts, Engineers, and Scientists with excellent academic and industrial experience in various disciplines of technologies (Biotechnology, Chemistry and Pharmaceuticals, Chemical Engineering, Food and Beverages, Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Plastics and Polymer Technology, Computer/Information Technology and Civil Engineering). Our team also includes highly experienced IPR Attorneys for handling legal matters involved with IPR and NON- IPR Attorneys for handling legal matters involved with non-IPR (other branches of law). Our team has expertise in analysing clients' requirements to provide practical and specialized technical/legal solutions by working collaboratively with each client in India and across the globe to understand and achieve clients' practical business approaches and goals successfully.

    Dr. Mohini K. Suryawanshi, Founder and Managing Partner
    Dr. Mohini K. Suryawanshi is founder and Managing Partner of the firm. She is an Indian lawyer, registered with Bar Council of India (BCI) and Bar Council of Maharashtra and Goa. She is doctorate in Biotechnology, a registered Indian Patent Agent and an IPR Attorney. She is awarded Women Scientist-C Fellowship from Dept. of Science and Technology (DST), India. She has over 11 years of extensive experience in dealing with Intellectual Property Rights (IPRs) Law in both domestic/Indian and overseas jurisdictions. She has worked in the area of IPR with reputed law firm named Krishna and Saurastri Associates, Bangalore; a private organisation named Avesthagen Ltd., Bangalore; and Govt. organisation of India. She has worked on different types/stages of IPRs including IPR Filing, IPR Prosecution, IPR valuation, IPR Commercialisation, IPR Portfolio Build-Up/IPR Portfolio Management by working actively and collaboratively with law firms/attorneys located in India and across the globe.

    IP Business Driven Services

    IP VALUATION: Customised techno-legal services to assess the value of IP assets and writing comprehensive technical, legal and commercial valuation reports.

    IP COMMERCIALIZATION: Customised techno-legal services for commercialisation and transfer of all types of Intellectual Property Rights (IPRs) via Licensing, Technology Transfer, Assignment and Transfer, Mortgaging, Franchising, Acquisition and Merger/Transmission and comprehensive Due Diligence.

    IP MANAGEMENT: Customised IP audit reports, drafting and negotiating legal documents such as Confidentiality and Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDA), Distribution Agreements, Collaboration Agreements, and Technology Transfer Agreements.
